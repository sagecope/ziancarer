Compementary Unit

Students who have a certificate of completion have been fully trained on how to be Caregivers. They can choose to go and work internationally in UK, US, Canada, Australia, etc. as long as they meet the work requirements of their employers and the countries, depending on available opportunities. Students can also choose to work locally in hospices, home care agencies, medical centers, clinics, hospitals, or be self employed as personal care or home care aides. ZTI also endeavours to keep all students especially those who excel on all upcoming opportunities both international and local. Alternatively, students can also choose to further their education by taking additional training or classes in the vast field of healthcare.

Full-Time

Our full-time classes run from 9am-5pm from Monday to Friday. Kindly note that upon acceptance students will be further advised of their schedule.

Part-Time Program

Our part-time classes run from 5pm-7pm Monday to Friday and Saturday Classes from 9am-4pm. Kindly note that upon acceptance students will be further advised of their schedule.

Students are strongly advised to keep class hours to prevent overlapping of class periods and to ensure they remain fully informed of their unit requirements. Class tardiness or absenteeism is not tolerated.
