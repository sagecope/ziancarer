WHAT IS Zian Caregiver Training Institute?

Zian Caregiver Training Institute is a premier training facility that offers competitive and comprehensive training in basic healthcare courses.

  1. Caregiving (Short term training with Certificate of Completion)
  2. Healthcare Assistant (National Certificate)
  3. Home Care Management  (National Certificate)
  4. Child Caregiver Course (National Certificate)

Our trainers are locally registered and licensed nurses with years of experience which ensures the training remains sensitive to the local needs but also meets international standards. Where possible we assist our students with internship placement and we also offer them post certification guidance on opportunities both locally and internationally depending on their desired future.
